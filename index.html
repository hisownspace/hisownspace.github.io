Portfolio

I'm a former farmer and systems-oriented thinker with a passion for coding.

During my brief but involved time as a programmer, I have built six full stack web applications. I have experience with Javascript, Python, SQL, Express, Flask, Redux, Sequelize, and SQLAlchemy.

I explored creating dynamic pages using vanilla javascript with my first app, which helped me understand the basis of what is happening under the hood of subsequent projects using React as a frontend library. Being able to successfully create, understand, and deploy applications using both Flask and Express helped me develop a holistic understanding of the abstract functionality of a backend server.

Working as part of a team in an agile environment helped me apply my understanding of cooperative efforts in a structured setting. I've also learned quite a bit about planting schedules.
